What is Reach?

Reach is the first and only social media club at USC. At Reach, we aim to create a community for influencers, digital marketers, and anyone interested in social media. During the semester, we feature creator workshops, influencer content days, USC student socials, guest speakers, and more. In the past, we've had guests from Google, TikTok, WarnerMedia, Paramount, and Fullscreen. We help give creation-focused students insight into the growing social media world and the world of content creation. We're more than just an org, we're a family!
